Resources FAQ

What are the best resources for preparing for maths exams?
Use platforms like Wolfram Alpha, Maths Genie, and practice with past papers.
How can I access past exam papers for ICT courses?
Visit your institution’s portal, the MISA resource page, or check with your department. You can also find official past papers on OCR.
Are there any recommended free coding platforms for beginners?
Yes! freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, and SoloLearn are great for beginners.
Where can I find additional learning materials for advanced mathematics?
Explore MIT OCW, Khan Academy, and arXiv.org for deeper math concepts.
