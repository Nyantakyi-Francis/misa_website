About MISA

Mathematics and ICT Students' Association - Mampong Technical College of Education

Our Mission & Purpose

To promote and protect the educational interest and welfare of members
To create awareness on the importance of using ICT in teaching and learning Mathematics
To help members understand the basics in ICT
To organize educational forums and professional development activities
To facilitate fundraising for association initiatives

Membership

All Maths and ICT department students are automatic members
The H.O.D of Mathematics and ICT serves as patron
Non-department students may join as associate members
Leaders must be level 200+ students with minimum 3.0 GPA

Association Leadership

MISA is managed by elected student leaders who meet twice monthly. Key leadership positions include:

President

  • Chief executive officer of MISA
  • Presides over all general meetings
  • Signatory to official documents and bank accounts

Vice President

  • Acts in the President's absence
  • Supports committee chairmen
  • Performs executive-assigned duties

General Secretary

  • Maintains official records and minutes
  • Manages association correspondence
  • Performs administrative duties

Financial Secretary

  • Maintains financial systems
  • Prepares financial statements
  • Bank account signatory

Treasurer

  • Records all financial transactions
  • Manages association funds
  • Maintains financial documentation

Organizing Secretary

  • Coordinates meetings and events
  • Plans social functions
  • Manages logistics
